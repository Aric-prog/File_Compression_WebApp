WHAT IS BSSC?

BSSC is an only organization which exists in BINUS Square. BSSC was established in 2010, the same year in which BINUS Square was opened. The main purpose of BSSC is to hold creative and innovative events that are able to develop the soft skills and hard skills of boarders who live in BINUS Square.

PARTNERSHIPS

EVENT

Our big events troughout the year are BSSC Earth Hour, BSSC Festival, BSSC Squarelympic, BSSC EXPO, and BSSC WONDER.

VISION & MISSION

To become an organization which is able to create and provide human resource that has global competitiveness and is able to play an active role in developing the inner talent and social awareness of boarders in 2020.

  1. Encouraging boarders to develop their inner potential through activities that BSSC provides.
  2. Realize the goals and desired activities of boarders.
  3. Improve the quality of BSSC members in order to raise the competitiveness of BSSC as an organization in global level.
